## Crossref Metadata Note

The Crossref record for Most et al. (2019) lists 59 references and includes the original abstract. That abstract explicitly frames the method as a cut, conditional-copy, paste workflow using high-resolution particle tracking trajectories to avoid dimensionality reduction, high-dimensional transition matrices, and loss of resolution important for mixing and reaction metrics.
